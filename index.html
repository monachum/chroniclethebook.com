Chronicle Book 3

Mist Taken

by Kevin Murphy
Dakkon’s in trouble. Things have gone horribly wrong.

But, so long as there’s still a glimmer of hope, it’s not too late, is it?

With such high stakes, failure isn’t an option. He’s got to stay crisp. Frosty. Focused.

Dakkon needs luck, skill, and a meteoric rise in power, however success won’t come so easily.

Nature is cruel. Predators come in all shapes and sizes, especially when the land is warped by magic.

Everything rests on Dakkon’s shoulders now, but perhaps someone can tell him:

Elves. Why must they all be so damned uppity?

About the author

Kevin is committed to developing deep, rich worlds beyond what's written on the page. Before he wrote tales of adventure, he was a fan of fantasy, sci-fi, and LitRPG/Gamelit. The influences of these genres are heavily present in his works.

Rather than tying a story to one, static location, Kevin strives to have his characters dive into fresh situations, discover new challenges, and uncover secrets hidden throughout the vast world. Most notably, he believes that books about adventure should involve a fair bit of adventuring.
